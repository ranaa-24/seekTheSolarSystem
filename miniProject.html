Solar System

The solar system is a mesmerizing collection of celestial objects that revolves around the Sun. Let's take a closer look at the Sun and the fascinating planets that call our solar system home.

  • The Sun:

    At the heart of our solar system lies the Sun, a gigantic ball of hot, glowing gas. It provides light, heat, and energy to all the planets within its gravitational pull.The Sun's immense size and powerful gravity keep the planets in orbit around it. It's essential for supporting life on Earth and serves as the ultimate source of energy for our planet.

  • Mercury:

    The closest planet to the Sun, Mercury is a rocky planet with a barren surface. It experiences extreme temperatures due to its proximity to the Sun.
